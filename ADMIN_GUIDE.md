## HOW TO EDIT THE CHATBOT

1. Log in to admin or staff portal
2. Click "Chatbot" in the sidebar menu
3. You will see all current chatbot responses

**To Add New Response:**
1. Click "Add Response" button
2. Fill in the form:
   - Keywords: Words that trigger this response (example: "fees, tuition, cost")
   - Response: The answer the chatbot will give
   - Category: General, Admissions, Programs, or Policies
   - Priority: Higher numbers appear first (use 1 for most responses)
3. Click "Save"

**To Edit Existing Response:**
1. Find the response in the table
2. Click the edit button
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Update"

**To Delete Response (Admin Only):**
1. Find the response in the table
2. Click the delete button (red trash icon)
3. Confirm deletion

---

## HOW TO ADD BLOG POSTS

1. Log in to admin or staff portal
2. Click "Blog Posts" in the sidebar menu
3. Click "Add New Post" button
4. Fill in the form:
   - Title: Blog post headline
   - Excerpt: Short summary (appears on blog listing page)
   - Content: Full article text
   - Author: Your name
   - Category: Events, Education, News, etc.
   - Read Time: Example "5 min read"
   - Image URL: Web address of header image
   - Featured: Check this to highlight the post
5. Click "Save"

**To Edit Blog Post:**
1. Find the post in the table
2. Click edit button
3. Make changes
4. Click "Update"

---

## HOW TO MANAGE PHOTO GALLERY

1. Log in to admin or staff portal
2. Click "Gallery" in the sidebar menu
3. Click "Add Image" button
4. Fill in the form:
   - Title: Photo caption
   - Description: Details about the photo
   - Image URL: Web address of the image
   - Category: Classroom, Events, Facilities, etc.
5. Click "Save"

**Note:** To upload images, you need to:
1. Upload the photo to a free image hosting service (Imgur, Cloudinary, etc.)
2. Copy the image URL
3. Paste it in the "Image URL" field
