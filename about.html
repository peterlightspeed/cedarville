Our Story

Growing Minds Since 2014

Cedarville Private School opened in 2014 with a vision to groom leaders into being the very best version of themselves. In June 2022, it was taken over by a new board in order to solve and meet certain demands of exceptional service. The new owners have a vision to create a system of grooming excellent leaders who would impact society at large.

By September 2022, a more excellent structure with basic amenities was created in the school. The curriculum then changed to the British and Nigerian curriculum which is in line with the vision to be a school that is renowned for excellence not just locally but globally.

At Cedarville, we care for children from as young as 3 months old to 11 years. We are a Cambridge International Examinations accredited school and our staff are highly qualified and experienced to take care of our children047s learning needs.
