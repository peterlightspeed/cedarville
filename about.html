About Cedarville Schools

Dedicated to nurturing young minds since 2014

Our Mission

To provide a world-class early childhood education that nurtures every child's intellectual, social, emotional, and physical development in a safe, loving, and stimulating environment.

Our Vision

To be the leading early childhood education institution in Lagos, recognized for producing confident, creative, and compassionate young leaders who positively impact their communities.

Our Story

A Decade of Nurturing Young Minds

Cedarville Private Schools was established in 2014 with a simple but powerful vision: to create an environment where every child feels safe, valued, and excited to learn.

What began as a small nursery with just a handful of students has grown into a fully-fledged early childhood institution offering Creche, Nursery, and Kindergarten programs to hundreds of children across Lagos.

Today, we continue to be guided by our founding principle — that every child deserves the best possible start in life.
