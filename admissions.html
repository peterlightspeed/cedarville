Admissions

Simple steps to get your child started at Cedarville

Enroll Today

We'd Love to Welcome Your Child

Enrolling at Cedarville is simple and straightforward. We guide parents through every step of the process to ensure a smooth and stress-free experience.

Our admissions are open throughout the year. We encourage early enrollment as spaces are limited to maintain our small class sizes and high-quality ratio of teachers to children.

How It Works

Our Admission Process

Four simple steps to enroll your child

1
Schedule a Tour

Visit our school and see our facilities, meet the teachers, and ask questions.

2
Submit Application

Fill out our enrollment form and submit the required documents.

3
Assessment

A brief and friendly assessment to place your child in the right class.

4
Confirmation

Receive your confirmation and welcome pack. Your child is ready to start!

What You Need

Admission Requirements

  • Birth Certificate – Official birth certificate of the child
  • Immunization Records – Up-to-date vaccination card
  • Passport Photographs – 2 recent passport-sized photos of the child
  • Parent ID – Valid ID of parent or guardian
  • Previous School Records – For Nursery and Primary transfers (if applicable)
